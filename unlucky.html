Cris-Sal, Ecuador's #1 brand of salt, wanted to be part of the World Cup qualifier. But, the Ecuadorian Football Federation didn't allow it. The reason? Ecuadorian culture believes salt brings bad luck.

If sponsoring Ecuador would make it lose, then sponsoring other countries' teams would make ours win. Cris-Sal became the "unofficial sponsor" of Ecuador's rivals.
