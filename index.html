This website is a canvas of simplicity - just a white background and grey letters.

Nothing more, nothing less.

A deliberate choice by us: José Serrano and José Reinoso. Two copywriters in a world dazzled by imagery. A world that always asks: And who is the art director?

And... sure. We could've jazzed things up, added some visuals here and there. But that might have meant roping in more hands, more cooks in the kitchen, possibly even muddling our message. We're action-driven, valuing common sense over convolution. And this website? It's a testament to that.

In the midst of visuals screaming for attention everywhere else, our site is a pause, a breath, a moment of simplicity. Because for us, good ideas don't need frills.

When words are crafted with care, who needs the glitter?
